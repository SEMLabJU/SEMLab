Principal Investigator

Dr. Rabindranath Gayen

Associate Professor | Department of Physics, JU

Dr. Gayen leads the SEM Lab, focusing on experimental condensed matter physics. His research group is dedicated to developing sustainable materials for high-efficiency solar cells and memory devices.

Research Interests:


  • Optoelectronic Materials and Devices
  • Resistive (ReRAM) Memory Applications
  • Oxide Semiconductor Nanostructures
  • Energy Materials
  • Kesterite Thin Films
  • Thin Film Solar Cells
  • 2D Materials
  • Polymer Nanocomposites
  • Supercapacitors
  • Triboelectric Nanogenerators (TENGs)
