WELCOME

Our research focuses on the seamless integration of energy conversion, harvesting, and storage technologies to drive sustainable energy solutions. Through innovative approaches, we aim to harness renewable energy sources efficiently, reduce reliance on fossil fuels, and mitigate environmental impacts. Join us in shaping a cleaner, greener future through our pioneering work in energy research.

The Lab

Chaired by Dr. Rabindranath Gayen, our lab investigates the intersection of solid-state physics and renewable energy. We specialize in the architectural design of thin-film semiconductors for photovoltaics and non-volatile memory devices.

Optoelectronic Devices

Our research explores the intersection of light and electricity, driving innovation through novel materials and advanced fabrication techniques. We strive to unlock new possibilities for energy conversion and sustainability.

Energy Storage

Advancing Supercapacitors poised to revolutionize how we store and utilize energy. Through rigorous experimentation and materials engineering, we aim to push the boundaries of storage technology.

Nanogenerators

Harnessing energy from the microscopic realm by converting mechanical motion at the nanoscale into electrical energy. These devices hold immense potential for environmental monitoring and portable electronics.
